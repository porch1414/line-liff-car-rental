## Idea A: "Midnight Velocity" — Dark Automotive Luxury

**Design Movement:** Dark Automotive / Supercar Showroom Aesthetic  
**Core Principles:**
- Deep charcoal + electric green accent — power and precision
- Asymmetric card layouts with diagonal cuts suggesting motion
- Heavy typographic contrast: ultra-bold display vs. light body text
- Glassy overlays and subtle grain texture for depth

**Color Philosophy:** Black (#0D0D0D) base with electric lime (#B8FF00) accents. Evokes speed, technology, and premium feel — like a luxury car showroom at night.

**Layout Paradigm:** Staggered card grid with offset hero. Left-aligned text blocks with right-side image bleeds. Bottom navigation bar for mobile.

**Signature Elements:**
- Diagonal slash dividers between sections
- Neon green CTA buttons with glow effect
- Car silhouette watermarks in section backgrounds

**Interaction Philosophy:** Snappy micro-animations — cards lift on hover, buttons pulse, transitions are fast and directional.

**Animation:** Slide-in from left for page transitions, scale-up for card hovers, green glow pulse on CTA.

**Typography System:** "Bebas Neue" for headings (ultra-condensed, bold) + "DM Sans" for body (clean, readable)

## Idea B: "Coastal Breeze" — Clean Scandinavian Mobile-First

**Design Movement:** Scandinavian Minimalism meets Southeast Asian Travel  
**Core Principles:**
- Off-white + deep teal + warm sand — calm, trustworthy, approachable
- Generous whitespace with intentional asymmetry
- Card-based layout optimized for thumb-zone interaction
- Soft shadows and rounded corners for a friendly, approachable feel

**Color Philosophy:** Background #FAFAF8 (warm white), primary #0F5F6D (deep teal), accent #F4A535 (warm amber). Evokes clear skies, coastal roads, and reliable service.

**Layout Paradigm:** Single-column mobile scroll with sticky bottom action bar. Horizontal scroll carousels for car listings. Floating profile avatar in header.

**Signature Elements:**
- Pill-shaped category filters
- Soft card shadows with teal left-border accent
- Animated LINE profile avatar integration

**Interaction Philosophy:** Smooth, unhurried transitions. Swipe gestures for carousels. Haptic-like button feedback via scale transforms.

**Animation:** Fade + slide-up for page load, spring physics for card interactions, smooth carousel scrolling.

**Typography System:** "Sora" for headings (geometric, friendly) + "Noto Sans" for body (excellent Thai/multilingual support)

## Idea C: "Urban Drive" — Bold Editorial Mobile App Feel

**Design Movement:** Bold Editorial / Neo-Brutalism lite  
**Core Principles:**
- Stark white + deep navy + vivid coral accent — confident and modern
- Full-bleed imagery with text overlays
- Strong typographic hierarchy with large section numbers
- Hard-edged cards with subtle border accents

**Color Philosophy:** White #FFFFFF base, navy #1A2744, coral accent #FF5C3A. Energetic, confident, urban — like a modern ride-hailing app.

**Layout Paradigm:** Full-width hero with overlay text. Two-column car grid on wider screens, single-column on mobile. Sticky header with search bar.

**Signature Elements:**
- Large section numbers as decorative elements
- Coral underlines on key headings
- Bold car name typography over image cards

**Interaction Philosophy:** Bold, decisive interactions. Tap animations that feel physical. Clear visual feedback on every action.

**Animation:** Staggered list entrance animations, bold scale transforms on tap, smooth page transitions.

**Typography System:** "Space Grotesk" for headings (distinctive, modern) + "Inter" for body (familiar, readable)

## Selected Design: **Idea B — "Coastal Breeze"**

Chosen for its mobile-first philosophy, warm approachability suited to a LINE ecosystem app, and excellent multilingual typography support (important for Thai LINE users). The teal + amber palette feels trustworthy and travel-ready without being generic.
